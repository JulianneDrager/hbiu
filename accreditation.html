HBIU: A State-Approved Religious Institution "In Accordance with The Florida State Department of Education Commission for Independent Education

HBIU is proud to be a fully registered University and Religious Institution. Under Florida Statute 1005.6 (1)(f) and Rule 6E-5.001 HBIU is recognized as an Independent Educational Organization given the right to offer educational programs and grant degrees in all of the following professional and vocational categories and more: Ministry, Counseling, Theology, Divinity, Education, Administration, Music, Fine Arts, Media, Communications, and Social Work. We are approved to grant Arts and Science Degrees on all levels such as Associate (A.A. & A.S.), Bachelor ( B.A. & B.S.), Master (M.A. & M.S.), and Doctorate (Ph.D., Ed.D). All of our programs are based on Religious and Professional Studies perspectives.

All of our programs are consistent with commission standards and we are committed to excellence and high quality in educational service.

Life Experience

All degrees at our college are earned degrees achieved by completing mandatory coursework. Our courses are available to everyone who meets our entrance requirements. Our courses require comprehensive study and self-discipline for those looking to earn online degrees. College transcripts do not always reflect real-life experiences. Our college recognizes life experiences that may have been gained through years of ministry-related work. Many people have served in ministry positions and have gained substantial knowledge and experience in their fields, but do not have any formal certificate, diploma, or degree, we also provide accelerated courses. Life experience credits can give some of the recognition needed. Applicants must have had more than five years in a recognized position in a faith-based humanitarian service to apply for life experience credits.
