Alumni

Alumni Association

HBIU Alumni Association membership consists of all certificates and degree recipients and former students having earned 15 credit hours or more at HBI University. There is an annual fee of $100 required to participate, and membership is automatic to all who qualify.

Association Board

The mission of the HBIU Alumni Association Board is to establish Honor, Become, Inspire and Unite. It is the goal of HBIU Alumni Association Board members to meet online monthly with members in the United States and globally.

The strategic plan of the HBIU Alumni Association Board covers three areas:

  • Sustain honor and strengthen the identity of the HBIU Alumni Association as a voice for all alumni
  • Inspire our alumni to Increase personal engagement with recent alumni
  • Increase alumni participation in HBIU Alumni Association activities

Alumni Association Board Membership

Each of the alumni board volunteers gives their time, talent, treasure, voice, and network to their alma mater. For more information about HBIU Alumni Association Board, e-mail: hbiualumni@gmail.com

The HBIU Alumni should function in the following order:

  • HBIU Alumni Association President
  • HBIU Alumni Association Vice President
  • HBIU Alumni Association Secretary
  • HBIU Alumni Association Treasurer
  • HBIU Alumni Association Public Relations Officer
  • HBIU Alumni Association Project Director

The President will preside in board meetings, oversee the Association, and ensure that goals are achieved.

The Vice President will assist the President in his roles, and head the Planning/Organizing Committee.

The Secretary will maintain membership records, execute basic clerical duties, and take minutes in the Planning/Organizing Committee.

The Treasurer serve as the Custodian of the funds in the Association, and will head the Fundraising Committee. The Public Relations Officer will ensure that the Association has an excellent public image by using a variety of marketing/public relations tools.

The Project Director will ensure that each project is properly executed.

Student Referral Committee. *Each committee will include floor members of the Association. *Committees are to meet on the last.

Tuesday of each month: *The President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Public Relations Officer, Project Director, and Student Referral Program Director will form the Association Board.

This Board will meet the first Tuesday of each month to discuss the reports of each committee.
